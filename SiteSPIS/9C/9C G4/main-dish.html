Main Dish

Pizza

Ingredients:

-110 ml water;

-1 teaspoon of sugar;

-10 g yeast;

-200 g of wheat flour plus a tablespoon (for sprinkling);

-10 ml olive oil;

-1 teaspoon salt;

-Toping(s) of choice;

  

Confection:

1. Place water, sugar and yeast in a bowl. Wrap gently until the yeast is diluted.

2. Add the oil, salt and flour. Knead with your hands until you get a smooth and homogeneous dough.

3. Cover the bowl and set aside at room temperature until it doubles in volume.

4. Place the dough on a flat surface sprinkled with flour, and it is ready to roll out with the desired shape.

5. After rolling the dough, place your desired topings.

6. Bake in the oven for 30 mins, and then serve.
